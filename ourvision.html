Our Vision Our vision at Canadian Robotics League is to maintain the technological standards required for the Canadian economy, through education and competitive spirit. CRL is an non-profit organization with a mission to provide universal access to STEAM education through our various outreach programs, and create a network of educational experiences from Newfoundland to Nunavut. Our organization is composed of the following principles: Provision, Education, Accessibility, and Professional Growth       Provision  Enabling individuals from all walks of life to gain valuable skills in STEAM education through various endeavours and CRL operations. Enabling individuals to gain valuable skills in the field(s) of robotics along with STEAM      Education  Giving teams, individuals, as well as mentors the ability to bestow valuable knowledge no matter the circumstance      Accessiblity Ensuring CRL can maintain the highest standards in robotics competition    Professional    Growth
